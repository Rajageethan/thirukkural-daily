About Thirukkural Daily

This is a personal project where I share one Thirukkural couplet each day, with its Tamil text, transliteration, translation, and explanations from scholars.

Thirukkural is a classic Tamil text with 1,330 couplets, offering timeless wisdom on ethics, wealth, and love. It is one of the most important works in Tamil literature.
